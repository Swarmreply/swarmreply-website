Questions, answered plainly

Frequently asked questions.

What is SwarmReply?
SwarmReply is AI reputation management software for local businesses. It automatically requests reviews after each job, routes unhappy customers to private feedback before they post publicly, drafts on-brand replies with AI, tracks your local SEO rankings, and monitors whether AI assistants like ChatGPT and Gemini recommend your business.
How is SwarmReply different from Birdeye or Podium?
We’re built for owner-operated businesses, not enterprises: pricing is published right here, plans are month-to-month with no annual contract, every feature is in the base plan, setup is self-serve in minutes, and AI search visibility tracking is included — a capability most traditional suites don’t offer.
How much does it cost?
$99/mo for your first location with every feature included. Additional locations are $79/mo each (2–5) and $69/mo each (6–25). No contracts, no setup fees, cancel anytime.
What is AI search visibility?
Customers increasingly ask AI assistants like ChatGPT and Gemini to recommend a business instead of searching Google. AI search visibility is knowing whether those assistants mention you when locals ask — and improving the reviews, ratings, and signals that make AI recommend you.
Do I need a contract or a demo call?
No. SwarmReply is self-serve and month-to-month. Sign up online in minutes and cancel anytime from your dashboard — no calls, no penalties.
What kinds of businesses use SwarmReply?
Local service businesses and storefronts: home services, HVAC and plumbing, dental and medical practices, restaurants, salons, auto repair, fitness studios, law firms — and the agencies that manage their marketing.
