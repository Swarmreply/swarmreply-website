Frequently asked questions

What is the SwarmReply webchat?

A chat widget you embed on your website so visitors can ask questions and reach you instantly. An AI agent, trained on your business, answers common questions 24/7 and hands off to you when it matters.

Does the AI agent know about my business?

Yes. You build a simple knowledge base — services, hours, policies, FAQs — and the agent answers from it. It won't make things up; when it doesn't know, it hands off to you.

What are the review widgets?

Embeddable widgets that showcase your best reviews and star rating directly on your website — social proof exactly where visitors decide whether to trust you. They update automatically as new reviews come in.

Can conversations turn into leads or reviews?

Yes. Webchat captures visitor details so no inquiry slips away, and SwarmReply can follow up to turn a great interaction into a review request — closing the loop from website visitor to happy reviewer.
